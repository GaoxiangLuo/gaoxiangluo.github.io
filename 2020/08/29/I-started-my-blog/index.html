I started my blog

Leon
2020-08-29
Log

Hey all! I finally started my own blog, since I’ve been captured by how elegant the markdown language is. There are some reasons for me to start blogging. Firstly, it motivates me to summarize the cool stuff that I’ve learned, which helps me to review and gain deeper insights of those knowledge. Secondly, I hope my blog will be a place where people can know me more; meanwhile, I would like to contribute my effort to the overall ComSci society, in publishing blogs that help people resolve technical issues. I hope it’s not too late to start as a rising junior undergrad, and I would like to see my blog grows as I grow!
